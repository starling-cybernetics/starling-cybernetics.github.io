Starling Cybernetics: Hello, World

Dear Reader,

Starling Cybernetics creates and maintains vital infrastructure enabling people and machines together to harmoniously participate in decentralized consensus.

Created and directed by me, finch (@plaidfinch), a founding core engineer for the Penumbra protocol, Starling Cybernetics is a proud member of the Penumbra ecosystem. Among other activities, we author and maintain the Penumbra governance dashboard and operate Starling Staking, an independent validator which will run from genesis upon the launch of the Penumbra mainnet.

In addition to validation and other infrastructure services, Starling Cybernetics also offers consulting and contracting for software projects both in and out of the blockchain space, drawing on our extensive background in applied cryptography and programming language design. If you're working on something interesting, we'd love to chat with you about it!

Drop us a line any time at contact@starlingcyber.net.
