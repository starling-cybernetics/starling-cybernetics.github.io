Starling Cybernetics: Hello, World

Dear Reader,

Starling Cybernetics creates infrastructure to empower communities of people and machines to harmoniously find collective consensus.

Starling Cybernetics is the creation of me, finch (@plaidfinch) — a founding core engineer for the Penumbra protocol — and is a proud independent member of the Penumbra ecosystem. We will operate the validator Starling Staking from the genesis of the Penumbra mainnet, and we are building and will maintain an interactive Penumbra governance dashboard, providing information about and enabling participation in on-chain democracy for Penumbra stakeholders.

In addition to these infrastructure services and public goods, Starling Cybernetics also offers consulting and contracting for software projects both related and unrelated to the blockchain, drawing on our extensive background in applied cryptography and programming language design. If you're working on something interesting, we'd love to chat with you about it! Drop us a line any time at consulting@starlingcyber.net.

For anything else, we love to get email at contact@starlingcyber.net.
