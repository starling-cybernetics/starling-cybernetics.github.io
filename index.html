Starling Cybernetics: Hello, World

Dear Reader,

Starling Cybernetics creates infrastructure for people and machines together to harmoniously find decentralized consensus.

Created and directed by me, finch (@plaidfinch), a founding core engineer for the Penumbra protocol, Starling Cybernetics is a proud independent member of the Penumbra ecosystem. Among other activities, we are authoring and will maintain the Penumbra governance dashboard, and will also operate Starling Staking, an active validator which will run from genesis upon the launch of the Penumbra mainnet.

In addition to these infrastructure services, Starling Cybernetics also offers consulting and contracting for software projects both related and unrelated to the blockchain, drawing on our extensive background in applied cryptography and programming language design. If you're working on something interesting, we'd love to chat with you about it! Drop us a line any time at consulting@starlingcyber.net.

For anything else, we love to get email at contact@starlingcyber.net.
